# Job helper agent
*By Ariane BOULET and Seraine TAWAMBA*

## Use Case
This agentic system will significantly increase your chances and save you time when applying to a new job. Job applicants today have a full-time job of curating their resume to each job offer, deliver a pertinent message with their resume to hope getting a reply from the recruiter, and preparing for their job interview. 

This agentic system will analyse the applicant's resume and the job description, bnut also the company, in order to produce a customised CV with the right keywords and information. It will also help the applicant curate a message for the recruiter and prepare them to the possible interview questions according to the desired position and the candidate's resume.
